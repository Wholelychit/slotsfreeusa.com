Coin systems explained

Sweeps Coins vs Gold Coins

Social casino-style sites often use more than one kind of coin. The names may change by site, but beginners usually need to understand one big idea: some coins are mainly for fun play, while other promotional coins may have separate sweepstakes-style rules.

Plain-English difference

Two coin types, two different jobs

Gold CoinsFun play

Often used for entertainment

Gold Coins are commonly used as virtual play credits. They may let visitors play slot-style games for entertainment, but they are not cash and should not be treated like money.

Sweeps CoinsPromotion rules

Often tied to sweepstakes-style rules

Sweeps Coins, Sweepstakes Coins, or similar names may work under promotional rules. Eligibility, location limits, redemption rules, and account requirements can vary.
