# SlotsFreeUSA SEO Checklist

## Technical SEO
- sitemap.xml exists
- robots.txt exists
- mobile-first layout
- fast loading pages
- clean URLs

## On-Page SEO
Each page should contain:
- one H1
- meta description
- readable title
- internal links
- footer links
- readable headings

## Content SEO
Focus on:
- educational guides
- beginner help
- mobile slot information
- bonus explainers
- safer play topics
- review pages

## Internal Linking
Connect:
- homepage
- guides
- reviews
- bonus pages
- responsible play pages

## Future SEO Goals
- schema markup
- FAQ schema
- article schema
- state pages
- comparison pages
- image optimization

## Content Quality Rules
- simple wording
- honest tone
- no fake promises
- no misleading claims
- entertainment focus only
