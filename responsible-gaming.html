Safety first

Responsible Play

Casino-style games should stay fun, calm, and optional. If play causes stress, money problems, relationship problems, or emotional harm, stop and get help.

Use Free Demo Only Understand Bonus Terms

SlotsFreeUSA does not offer real-money gambling, accept bets, process payments, or issue payouts.

Stop signs

Warning Signs

  • Chasing losses or trying to win back money.
  • Hiding play, spending, or account activity.
  • Borrowing money or using bill money to play.
  • Feeling stress, anger, shame, or panic after playing.
  • Ignoring work, family, sleep, or health because of play.
Better habits

Safer Play Habits

  • Set a time limit before starting.
  • Set a spending limit before joining any outside site.
  • Take breaks and walk away when frustrated.
  • Never treat slot-style games as income.
  • Use free-play demos when possible.

When to get help

Get help if gambling or casino-style play is causing harm, debt, secrecy, conflict, anxiety, depression, or loss of control.

In the United States, people can contact the National Problem Gambling Helpline at 1-800-GAMBLER. If someone is in immediate danger or may hurt themselves, call emergency services right away.

For family and friends

If someone you care about is struggling, stay calm. Avoid insults or shame. Encourage them to pause play, protect money, and contact professional help.
