Feature Terms

Wilds, scatters, and bonus rounds

A wild symbol can sometimes stand in for another symbol. A scatter may trigger a feature in some games. A bonus round is an extra game feature that may include picks, spins, or other rules.

These rules change by game, so do not assume every slot works the same way.

Offer Terms

Free spins and playthrough

Free spins may be part of a game feature or outside offer. Playthrough, sometimes called rollover, describes activity that may be required before promotional value can be used under site rules.
