# SlotsFreeUSA Affiliate Follow-Up Calendar

Last updated: 2026-05-28

Use this file to plan one polite follow-up 5 to 7 business days after an affiliate application, contact form, or partner email is sent.

Do not send repeated follow-ups that look spammy. Do not add live affiliate links, tracking links, ad scripts, payment setup, or private credentials while waiting for replies.

## Follow-Up Rules

1. Record the original send date in `AFFILIATE-SUBMISSION-LOG.md`.
2. Choose a follow-up date 5 to 7 business days later.
3. Send only one polite follow-up unless the partner replies and asks for more information.
4. If the partner rejects the application, mark it rejected and stop outreach for that partner.
5. If the partner approves the application, complete `AFFILIATE-APPROVAL-CHECKLIST.md` before any link goes live.
6. Keep Caesars on hold until an official verified affiliate path is found.

## Business Day Guide

Business days are Monday through Friday. Skip Saturday and Sunday. If a holiday is known, choose the next reasonable business day.

Example: if an application is sent on Thursday, May 28, 2026, the 5 to 7 business day follow-up window is Thursday, June 4, 2026 through Monday, June 8, 2026.
