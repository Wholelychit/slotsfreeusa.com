Common bonus types

Words visitors should understand first

Start Here

Welcome Bonuses

First-time offers can sound simple, but the details matter. Always check eligibility, time limits, location rules, and what action is required.

Common Offer

Free Spins

Some offers include spins instead of cash value. Check which games qualify, how long the spins last, and whether winnings or credits have limits.

Social Casino

Sweepstakes Coins

Social casinos may use different coin systems. Read the official terms so you know what each coin type means and what rules apply.

Do Not Skip

Playthrough Rules

Playthrough or rollover terms explain what must happen before promotional value can be used under site rules. This is where many offers become confusing.

Plain-English checklist

Before You Claim Any Offer

✅ Confirm the offer is available in your state or location.

✅ Read the terms before signing up.

✅ Check age requirements and identity verification rules.

✅ Look for playthrough, expiry, game restrictions, and maximum redemption details.

✅ Check whether a purchase, deposit, mailing method, or account verification is required.

✅ Set a limit before play starts and treat every offer as entertainment.

Bonus red flags

Be careful when an offer sounds too easy

WarningToo much hype

Guaranteed winning language

A real offer should not promise guaranteed winnings, easy money, or a secret way to beat slot-style games.

WarningMissing terms

No clear rules page

If rules, expiry dates, limits, and restrictions are hard to find, slow down before signing up.

WarningPressure

Rush language

Offers that push fast action without clear terms can lead to bad choices. Read first, then decide calmly.
