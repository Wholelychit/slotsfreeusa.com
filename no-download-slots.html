Quick no-download slot answers

What are no-download slots?

No-download slots are slot-style games that run in a browser without a separate app or desktop program.

Are no-download slots always free?

No. Some are free-play demos, while outside casino-style websites may include purchases, deposits, account rules, or promotional terms.
