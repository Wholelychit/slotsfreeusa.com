Simple answers

Best online slots FAQ

What type of slot is best for beginners?

Simple slots with clear rules, readable symbols, and free-play mode are usually easier for beginners than complex feature-heavy games.

Does a higher RTP guarantee better results?

No. RTP is a long-term estimate and does not predict short sessions or the next spin.

Should I choose high-volatility slots?

Not automatically. High volatility can mean bigger possible wins, but it can also mean longer dry spells and more stress.
